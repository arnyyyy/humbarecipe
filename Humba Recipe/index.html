HUMBA RECIPE

Ingredients:

Name Quantity
Pork belly 1.5 kg
Soy sauce 1/2 cup
vinegar 1/4 cup
brown sugar 3 tbsp
garlic 2 cloves
onion 2 pcs
bay leaves 2 pcs
salt 1/2 tsp
black pepper 1 tsp
pineapple juice 1 cup
cooking oil 2 tbsp

Steps:

  • Step 1: Pan fry 1.5 kilo pork liempo sliced into large cube on
    thin layer of soil on medium flame setting.
  • Step 2: Add 1/2 tsp salt and ground black pepper.
  • Step 3: Continue to pan fry on medium flame setting until
    fat is rendered and meat becomes golden brown.
  • Step 4: When fat is rendered and the meat is golden brown
    in color, set aside.
  • Step 5: Saute chopped 2 pcs onions on rendered fat on
    high flame setting for about 20secs.
  • Step 6: Add 6 cloves chopped garlic and continue to saute
    on high flame setting for another 10 sec.
  • Step 7: Add the panfried pork and continue to saute on
    high flame setting
    for another 1 min.
  • Step 8: Add 1 cup pineapple juice (unsweetened), mix
    thoroughly and set the flame on medium setting.
  • Step 9: Turn off the fire and serve it on a bowl.
